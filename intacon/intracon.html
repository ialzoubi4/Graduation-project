EduTrack Domain
The system is designed to support Data Science students and department head as well in managing the educational Process and analyzing educational data, such as student performance, skills development and etc.
EduTrack Effect
The system has a great impact on the department in general and on students in particular, It aims to improve the educational process effictively and building a generation with high practical experience and capable of competing in the labor market against colleagues from other universities.
EduTrack Data-Sync
Data synchronization in this project ensures that all components of the system such as student performance records, course enrollment data, skills assessments, and visual analytics are consistently updated and reflects the most recent information.
EduTrack Privacy
The system ensures the privacy of the personal and academic information of every person considered as a stakeholder in the department.
